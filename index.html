ALLIGATOR HUNT EVENT
// RECURRING ALLIANCE BOSS EVENT — EVERY 48 HOURS //
What is it? Alligator Hunt is a recurring alliance rally event every 48 hours where your alliance groups up to attack a giant alligator boss. It rewards resources and weapon upgrade materials — some of the best F2P material income in the game.

📋 EVENT OVERVIEW

BASIC INFO
  • TYPEAlliance rally boss event
  • FREQEvery 48 hours
  • SIZEFull alliance participation
  • TIMERBoss despawns after 30 minutes
  • COSTNo action points / stamina required
KEY REWARDS
  • DROPWeapon upgrade materials
  • DROPResource packages
  • DROPAlliance contribution points

🐊 HOW TO PARTICIPATE

  1. Check the event timer Go to the Events tab and find the Alligator Hunt countdown. The event spawns the boss on the map at fixed intervals.
  2. Coordinate with your alliance Use alliance chat to agree on rally timing. The boss requires multiple simultaneous rallies to kill efficiently — attacking together deals far more damage than spreading out.
  3. Set your Rally team Use your best combat heroes. Luis (AoE damage) + Billy (DEF shred) lead the highest-damage rallies. Enable the "Join Rally" stat heroes as Squad Leader for the bonus.
  4. Launch rallies The alliance leader or officers start the rally. Other members join before the countdown ends. The more members join, the more damage — and the faster the kill.
  5. Claim your rewards After the Alligator dies, rewards are distributed. Higher damage contributions = better personal reward tier.
  6. Heal your troops After the rally, check your hospital. Troops injured in the battle need to be healed — infected troops can spread if not treated.

💡 TIPS & TRICKS

🏆 Max your rewards: Join EVERY rally your alliance starts, even if your contribution is small. Alliance participation bonuses are shared — every member gets a cut of the total kill reward.
🎯 Use Aberration-killing heroes: Some heroes have the "Aberration" combat trait. These get bonus stats when fighting B.O.W. bosses like the Alligator. Check the hero's trait tab before setting your rally team.
⏱️ The gator always dies: The boss automatically despawns after 30 minutes regardless — it will always be killed. Focus on joining as many rallies as possible to maximise your personal reward contribution.
📅 Alligator Hunt & Ace's Proof Day 6: On ACE Day 6 (Defeat Infected/Aberrations), killing the Alligator in alliance rallies gives 90,000 ACE points per rally — by far the most efficient way to score on that day.

💡 RALLY TIPS

Auto-Rally is essential: Enable auto-rally in settings. This automatically joins your squad to alliance rallies even when you're offline. Missing rallies means missing alliance points, rewards, and contribution rankings.
🏆 Best Rally Leaders — Leon, Rebecca or Tyrell: These three heroes have the strongest Field skills that boost the most damage for the entire rally. Leon's ATK garrison bonus, Rebecca's sustain buffs, and Tyrell's field boost all amplify every joining squad. Always put your best-invested of these three as Rally Leader for offensive rallies.
Rally Joiner rule: Only your top-right Field skill matters when joining a rally. Always check your hero's Field skill before marching — wrong skill type = wasted march.
Shorter rally timers for PvP: When attacking player bases, shorter timers (1–2 min) reduce the time the target has to reinforce, rally defend, or relocate. Don't give them 5 minutes to prepare.
⚠️ Heal troops after every rally: Troops injured in rallies must be hospitalized immediately. Infected troops can spread the infection to healthy troops if left untreated — this can wipe out your army faster than the enemy.
Contribution points matter: Higher rally contribution = higher ranking in alliance events. Even joining mid-rally with weak troops adds up over time. Participation consistency is rewarded more than one-off big hits.
